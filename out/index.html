The Campus Ambassador Program is a student-led initiative where chosen ambassadors champion our brand, coordinate events, and gather feedback from their campuses. The goal of this program is to build a robust community and increase brand visibility among students.

Current university or college students with strong communication skills and a genuine enthusiasm for Antaragni are encouraged to apply.

You can apply by registering on our website. The application process requires just submitting your details.

The number of campus ambassadors selected will vary based on the volume of applications received.

Shaurya is the annual inter-collegiate sports fest of IIT Kharagpur, which is held over a span of three days, typically in the month of October. It was started in the year 2008 with a vision of promoting sports culture, and providing a national platform for athletes and sportsmen. Shaurya witnessed four editions, in the years 2008, 2009, 2010 and 2012.
